FAQ

Questions & Answers

In English and Hindi — whichever is easier for you.

What is the turnaround time?
Standard videos are delivered in 3–5 business days. Wedding films take 7–14 days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
What video formats do you accept?
All major formats — MP4, MOV, ProRes, RAW, BRAW. You can share files via Google Drive, WeTransfer or Dropbox.
How does payment work?
50% upfront before work begins, 50% upon final delivery. I accept UPI, Bank Transfer, PayPal and Wise.
How many revisions do I get?
Revisions are included until you're satisfied. My goal is 100% client satisfaction on every project.
Do you offer monthly retainers?
Yes! Monthly retainer packages are available for creators and brands who need consistent editing. You get priority turnaround and dedicated support.
Video kitne time mein ready hogi?
Standard videos 3–5 business days mein deliver hoti hain. Wedding films ke liye 7–14 din lagte hain. Rush delivery extra charge pe available hai.
Footage kaise share karein?
Google Drive, WeTransfer ya Dropbox se share kar sakte hain. Koi bhi format chalega — MP4, MOV, ProRes, RAW.
Payment kaise hogi?
50% advance project start karne se pehle, 50% final delivery par. UPI, Bank Transfer ya PayPal se payment ho sakti hai.
Agar pasand na aaye toh?
Revisions tab tak karenge jab tak aap satisfied na ho jaayein. 100% client satisfaction meri priority hai.
Long-term kaam ho sakta hai?
Bilkul! Monthly retainer plans available hain — priority delivery aur dedicated support ke saath. WhatsApp pe baat karein details ke liye.
